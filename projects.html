Urban Interaction Design

October 2017 Groupwork (2)


The Socotra Tree is a concept for a structure that has overarching objectives of smart cities. The Socotra Tree serves as a standing working station and consists of an intricate design encasing the structure which is essential for the guidance of wind toward the turbine, allowing for a more efficient generation of this form of renewable energy. The turbine utilises a curved and slanted design to maximise safety precautions for people and wildlife.
